About Ice Cubes LLC

When it comes to maintenance, installs, or cleanings during business hours, Ice Cubes excels at efficiency and invisibility. We will not compromise any aspect of a functioning business while performing a repair, install, or cleaning. As a preferred vendor for several fast-food chains, Ice Cubes understands the importance of commitment to your customer. Like the restaurants we serve, as they are committed to their customers, as are we to their needs.

With a flexible and detail-oriented staff, we are centered on the needs of the businesses we serve. We strive to be as invisible as possible when working in a functioning kitchen, and to do the job right the first time, so that there is no unnecessary down-time. Our experienced technicians spot potential problems before they develop, saving time and money for our clients every step of the way.
